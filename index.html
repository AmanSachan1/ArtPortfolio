About

I chase the same idea across clay, ink, and code — that making is thinking made visible.

I'm Aman Sachan, an artist and graphics engineer who refuses to pick a single medium. My days move between a pottery wheel, a drawing tablet, and a code editor full of shaders — and I've come to believe each one teaches the others.

Ceramics keeps me honest about form and patience. Illustration keeps my hand loose. Real-time graphics let me build worlds that move. The thread running through all of it is curiosity: I'd rather make something a little messy and alive than something polished and silent.
